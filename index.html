About Us

Welcome to my website! I’m a 14-year-old passionate about the art of crafting Damascus knives, where each piece tells a story through its intricate patterns and sharp functionality. My journey into this fascinating world of metallurgy has ignited a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and creativity. In addition to my work with knives, I’m also diving into the world of coding. I’m currently learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build engaging websites, and I’m exploring Python to enhance my programming skills. I believe that combining these interests allows me to express myself in unique ways. On this site, I’ll share my projects, techniques, and experiences, as well as the challenges and triumphs I encounter along the way. Thank you for joining me on this exciting journey of art and technology!
